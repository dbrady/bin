This dah-dah, dah-dah, dadadah, dadadah rhythm pushes fast, flicky struming.

TIP: Use the "flicking water off your hand" motion; you have to strum full
chords so try not to anchor your picking hand on the bridge. That's absolutely a
good technique to use for stability, but for high-speed picking it locks your
arm into elbow-driven strums.
